Weekly Pipeline Performance Dashboard

Executive dashboard giving sales leadership full visibility into pipeline volume, revenue potential, owner performance, stage distribution, modality mix and priority HOT opportunities — with week-over-week comparison and YTD views.

Revenue Ops HTML · JavaScript
The problem

Sales teams had no single view of pipeline health. Leadership relied on manual spreadsheet consolidation weekly — slow, error-prone and never fully up to date.

What I built
  • 6 executive KPIs with live delta vs prior week
  • 7 filters: division, branch, owner, stage, modal, market, HOT flag

Projects Operations Control Tower

End-to-end operational dashboard for project cargo tracking — covering the full container journey from origin to final delivery, with demurrage exposure, incident monitoring and branch-level concentration analysis.

Operations HTML · JavaScript
The problem

Operational data was scattered across multiple Excel tabs. Project managers had no consolidated view of container status, demurrage exposure or delivery progress by client.

What I built
  • 5-tab dashboard: Overview, Journey, Tracker, Incidents, Demurrage
  • End-to-end journey from origin → customs → transit → delivery

Operations Shipment Tracker — FCL · AIR · LCL

Unified shipment performance dashboard consolidating three modalities (Sea FCL, Air, Sea LCL) with 2025 vs 2026 year-over-year comparison, branch-level analysis and IMPO/EXPO breakdown.

Logistics Ops HTML · JavaScript
The problem

Three separate Excel dashboards for FCL, AIR and LCL. No consolidated view, no YoY comparison, no branch-level breakdown. Manual work every week to see the full picture.

What I built
  • Single dashboard reading 3 separate Excel files
  • Volume KPIs: TEU (FCL), CBM (LCL), KG (Air)
  • 2025 vs 2026 comparison with % delta on every metric
  • 9 branches with full names mapped (CPQ → Campinas etc.)
  • IMPO vs EXPO split, weekly trends, modal mix
Outcome

Replaced 3 manual weekly Excels with one live dashboard. Operations managers gained instant visibility into volume trends, branch concentration and year-over-year performance.

CRM Process Optimization & Pipeline Governance

End-to-end CRM redesign — from fragmented workflows to a structured, governed pipeline with standardized stages, data validation, automation and reporting that leadership could actually rely on.

RevOps / CRM Dynamics 365 · HubSpot
The problem

What I did
  • Mapped and standardized the full lead → close funnel

Outcome

↑ 15–25% early-stage conversion. ↓ 20% deal stagnation time. Forecast reliability improved significantly. ~30% reduction in manual admin workload across the team.

Revenue Forecasting & Pipeline Health Reporting

Structured forecasting routine and pipeline health framework connecting CRM data to commercial leadership decisions — making weekly reviews faster, more focused and more actionable.

Forecasting Excel · Power BI
The problem

No structured forecasting routine. Revenue reviews were reactive, based on incomplete data. Sales managers spent more time building the report than acting on it.

What I built
  • Weekly pipeline review template with stage-weighted forecast
  • Conversion rate tracking by stage, owner and division
  • Deal aging alerts to flag stagnant opportunities early
  • Forecast vs actual tracking over rolling 4-week windows
  • Executive summary slide auto-generated from the data
Outcome

Leadership moved from reactive to proactive pipeline management. Forecast meetings became shorter and more decision-focused. Visibility into at-risk deals increased significantly.
